7 Markets That Make Coimbatore Worth Visiting!

Coimbatore, often referred to as Kovai is a city in Tamil Nadu, India's western region. It is one of India's fastest-growing cities and is well-known for its manufacturing, education, healthcare, and textile industries. Coimbatore is a great place to go shopping because there are so many options available at reasonable pricing. There are numerous marketplaces and shopping centres in the city that serve the requirements of both residents and visitors.

1. RS Puram Market

RS Puram Market is one of Coimbatore's oldest markets and is well-known for its textiles and ethnic wear. A wide selection of cotton and silk sarees, dress supplies, and dhotis are available at affordable costs.

Location: The market is located in Coimbatore's RS Puram neighbourhood, close to the RS Puram Post Office. Public transportation is simple to utilize, with several buses and taxis available from various locations throughout the city.

  • Clothing: The RS Puram Market is home to a number of stores that sell sarees, salwar kameez, and kurtas, among other traditional Indian garments. T-shirts, pants, and dresses among other contemporary clothing items are also available.
  • Handicrafts: The market is renowned for its handicrafts, which include wooden objects, pottery, and handcrafted jewellery. You can find one-of-a-kind and lovely mementoes to bring home.
